Выучить и рассказать про Псков
Pskov is known as one of the ancient town.The first mention of Pskov was in 903. It was founded on the banks of the rivers Velikaya and Pskova. The history of Pskov is Victorious. The town survived 26 sieges. During the 13-16th centuries Pskov was a cultural centre. In monasteries the historical Chronicles were written.

Architecture and icon painting schools were formed in Pskov.

Now Pskov is not a very large town. The heart of the town is Kremlin with trinity cathedral. There are a lot of orthodox churches in Pskov. In spaso - mirozhskiy monastery there is a beautiful ensemble.

You can see monuments to Princess olga, to Alexander Pushkin. In 2013 our town was chosen one of the symbols of Russia
